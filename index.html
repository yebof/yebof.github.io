About Yebo

I am a second-year Ph.D. student of Professor Jun Li in the Center for Cyber Security and Privacy, University of Oregon. Currently, my research topics include computer security, anomaly detection, DDoS defense, and data analysis. I received my B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Yangzhou University in 2016. After that, I got M.S. degree in Computer Science at University of Oregon in 2018.
