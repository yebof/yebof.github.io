About Yebo

I am a second-year Ph.D. student of Professor Jun Li in the Center for Cyber Security and Privacy, University of Oregon. Currently, my research topics include computer security & privacy, machine learning and data analysis, and anomaly & intrusion detection. I received my B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Yangzhou University in 2016. After that, I got M.S. degree in Computer Science at University of Oregon in 2018.
